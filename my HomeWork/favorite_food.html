Ice cream

Ice cream is a yummy dessert and to make delicious vanilla ones you will need: condensed milk, whipped cream and a biscuit cone.

Ingredients:

  • Condensed milk;
  • Whipped cream;
  • Biscuit cone;
  • Vanilla flavor;
  • A person.
  1. Mix the condensed milk with the whipped cream in a mixer.
  2. When you are done, you take your mixture and the vanilla flavor and put it in a contenant that you want to let the ice cream inside. Let it in the freezer
    3. When it's frozen let it outside for a bit until you are able to cut it easly and serve it.
    4. I said you need a person. That means someone to tast it! Now thats how we make vanilla ICE CREAM!
